Are you interested in being on the forefront of Microsoft’s connected sales and marketing transformation? Are you passionate about business process and operations, and the ability to influence the future of how we connect with our current and future customers? If so, the Global Demand Center (GDC) team is the place for you! 

The Global Demand Center team is looking for Business Process and Operations Manager for our lead management and routing efforts on our regional GDC team. The Regional GDC Operations team will help scale Microsoft’s Global Demand center, bridging local and corporate partners, initiatives, and execution across the GDC, Microsoft’s field sales, marketing teams, and inside sales. In this role, you will partner closely with both Corp and the inside sales organization to identify areas to optimize and accelerate lead prioritization and routing, making lead routing more efficient for the Asia region, including APAC, Australia, Japan, GCR, and India. You will also work with the Regional GDC lead to help set forecasting, as well as optimize overall volume/capacity with the insides sales team. 

You excel at cross team collaboration, work well with ambiguity, and have a history of solving tough problems in complex environments; as well as demonstrating leadership, influence, and impact skills. Your strong business insight and deep analytical skills will contribute to your success.

#Responsibilities

RESPONSIBILITIES INCLUDE:
- Monitor lead volumes vs forecast and set capacity planning based on campaign priorities and performance, with emphasis on local area lead contribution
- Troubleshoot lead flow issues and alert and triage with core teams 
- Identify areas to streamline lead handoff process to accelerate overall lead flow process
- Bridge lead quality feedback from regional inside sales teams upstream to Corp team 
- Use lead scoring model to prioritize lead routing to Inside Sales and/or Partner
- Act as an extension of Corp on any locally – enacted GDC Services (eg accelerated lead routing)

#Qualifications

Skills/Qualifications:
- College degree (MBA/Masters preferred) 8-10 years of relevant work experience in sales and marketing or business planning and operations. 
- Self-starter with a commitment to succeed in a quickly changing business environment
- Strong time and project management skills with proven track record of successfully running multiple projects simultaneously
- Experience working with executive level managers required
- Comfort with high levels of ambiguity and ability to have impact & influence
- Strategic thinker with strong analytical skills with the ability to synthesize key insights into actionable program recommendations
