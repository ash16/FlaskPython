#OVERVIEW

As part of our transformation, one of our key areas of focus is the modernization of our sales motions. The Insides Sales organization is a newly formed organization with a charter to accelerate Microsoft’s growth in its cloud-first, mobile-first businesses along with the traditional businesses. This organization is at the forefront of establishing a new sales model leveraging modern technology and big data and analytics to drive impactful demand response and targeted sales coverage.

Do you possess superior leadership, communications and consultative capabilities?
As a Licensing Manager, you will work directly with customers, your sales team and implementation partners to achieve quarterly and annual sales quotas - closing net-new business, as well as managing and growing Microsoft's existing book of business. This role has significant business impact through direct involvement in customer/partner licensing opportunities. The Manager will engage in solving complex business problems and driving business growth while partnering with field leaders in delivering licensing solutions focused on the needs of partners and customers.

#RESPONSIBILITIES

• Business partnering with business group and field leaders and driving licensing and negotiating management process for Licensing Sales Specialists in the Inside Sales organization in Australia and Asia Pacific.
• Leadership responsibilities for group of Licensing Sales Specialists and Customer Lifecycle Managers that work directly with other sales teams, partners and customers to close long term Microsoft licensing agreements. 
• Develop and evolve licensing strategy; drive negotiation and deal strategy with field teams on complex business and legal situations.
• Provide insightful analyses to field and business group leaders, as it relates to negotiated deals - win/loss analytics, channel and pricing and licensing model analytics. 
• Enhance, streamline, and maintain global licensing processes while adapting to market trends and driving Inside Sales business performance improvement.
• Build and maintain strong working relationships across multiple groups at Microsoft - Worldwide Licensing & Pricing, Corporate, SMSG/Field segments and regional teams, Business Group product management and finance, Operations - and with other key partners across the company.
