#Responsibilities

The Impact You’ll Be Making… 

As an Account Technology Strategist, you are focused on helping our Federal Government Public Sector Social Services clients to achieve more. This an opportunity to help our customers reimagine technology in their organisation and in service of the Australian Citizen. Your role as the Account Technology Strategist is that of a virtual Chief Technology Officer with a strong focus on technical engagement and leadership with our customer’s business and IT decision makers. 
As an Account Technology Strategist, you will add value by: 
• Having a solid understanding of the Public Sector Federal Government, Social Services and related industries. 
• Demonstrating and providing thoughtful points of view on the transformative role Digital Technologies plays in citizen centric services related agencies. 
• Building trusted relationships with customer decision makers, including Chief Technology and Information Officers as well as business decision makers. 
• Sharing your broad knowledge of the Microsoft Platform, our competition, and the industry to create a vision of how Microsoft can be the customer’s best partner to help deliver their business outcomes. 
• Gathering and sharing broad insights about customers and the IT Technology landscape with your Microsoft colleagues. 
• Demonstrating technical leadership by working with your team and Microsoft resources to leverage Microsoft Cloud Technologies to drive customer success. 
• Promote the long-term growth of Microsoft through the adoption and consumption of our cloud solutions. 
• Working with and leading business partners into the territory to drive the best possible outcomes for our customers. 

Microsoft Account Technology Specialists are unique in their: 
• Broad knowledge of the Microsoft enterprise platform and ability to map relevant Microsoft technologies to the customers’ IT and business needs and priorities. 
• Ability to build long-term trusted technology relationships that result in solid and meaningful strategic technology conversations. 
• In-depth knowledge of customers’ technology environments. 
• Ability to ensure that customers benefit from the latest Microsoft innovations, solutions and thought leadership. 
• Focus on long-term, sustainable growth.

• Extensive experience working within teams is required. 
• Strong written and verbal communication skills are required.

Education 
• Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or a similar information technology-related discipline required. Master’s degree preferred.
